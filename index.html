Welcome to Endangered Species Memory Game

This project is aimed at people of any age or religious belief. Because we all have to unite to save those who are most vulnerable to the descrtuctive nature of the mankind. Thousands of species are in brink of extinction because of our exploits. Think a bit more of what surrounds us and enjoy the game.

How to Play:

The rules are quite simple. You need to match all the cards with their pair. And try to do in the least amount of turns possible. The best players will be shown on the leaderboards.

Here you can enter your name, please note that it will be used for leaderboards and you won't be able to change it later.
